Diddit!

Diddit! is a combination habit tracker / to-do list / personal journal inspired by bullet journals. Bullet journaling helps me keep track of my day-to-day responsiblities and helps me feel like I'm on top of my usually extremely busy life, but mapping out the calendars and notes and daily habits for each month was time consuming and I'd often put it off and then fall behind on my journaling. I created Diddit! because it's something I'd want to use myself, and I designed it with an accesibile user interface and validating experience in mind.

Diddit! utilizes Ruby and Ruby on Rails for the backend, and JavaScript and VueJS on the frontend. I'm excited to keep working on the app and see it through to a fully usable application.
